About Me

I'm a native Floridian living in Orlando but I've lived in a lot of other places, too: Germany, Italy, Colombia, Texas, North Carolina, Arizona, Virginia. I'm currently a project manager for a call center broker -- call center is an ugly business and I can't wait to get out!


I'm the secretary/treasurer for the Florida State Horseshoe Pitcher's Association. I took on the job because I can't say "no" to my Grandpa. Now, seven years have passed and I can't believe I'm still doing it! FSHPA has the worst website ever: FSHPA.com
